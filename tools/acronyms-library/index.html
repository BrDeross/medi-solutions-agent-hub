Insurance Acronyms Library

Search Medicare, life insurance, annuity, and group insurance acronyms.

AEP
Annual Election Period — Oct 15 to Dec 7 Medicare plan change window.
IEP
Initial Enrollment Period — 7-month window around turning 65.
SEP
Special Enrollment Period — triggered by qualifying life events.
IRMAA
Income Related Monthly Adjustment Amount — income-based Medicare premium surcharge.
LIS
Low Income Subsidy — extra help for Part D drug costs.
MOOP
Maximum Out of Pocket — spending cap on Medicare Advantage plans.
PDP
Prescription Drug Plan — stand-alone Medicare Part D drug coverage.
MAPD
Medicare Advantage Prescription Drug plan.
SNP
Special Needs Plan — Medicare Advantage plan for certain groups.
HMO
Health Maintenance Organization.
PPO
Preferred Provider Organization.
WL
Whole Life insurance.
UL
Universal Life insurance.
IUL
Indexed Universal Life insurance.
VUL
Variable Universal Life insurance.
AD&D
Accidental Death and Dismemberment insurance.
ROP
Return of Premium.
FIA
Fixed Indexed Annuity.
MYGA
Multi-Year Guaranteed Annuity.
SPIA
Single Premium Immediate Annuity.
DIA
Deferred Income Annuity.
RMD
Required Minimum Distribution.
ACA
Affordable Care Act.
MEC
Minimum Essential Coverage.
SBC
Summary of Benefits and Coverage.
ERISA
Employee Retirement Income Security Act.
FPL
Federal Poverty Level.
